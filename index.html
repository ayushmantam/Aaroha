Welcome To "Aaroha"



Aaroha” came into existence with the thought of providing free basic education and a nurturing environment for the overall growth and development of the weaker and less privileged classes of our society. As the name suggests, the organization is committed to the cause of enlightening the lives of the poor children and driving them away from the darkness of illiteracy and ignorance. Aaroha is not a group of wealthy and resourceful people. Instead they are the students of various govt. and private colleges of Bhopal who have different aspirations, dreams and ambitions in life but share a common vision.

Our Projects

AKSHAR

Project Akshar aims to educate underprivileged children at different centres.
It imparts them with elementary knowledge before they acquire enough competence to be admitted in various private institutions.

PAATHSHALA

Works to enable meritorious students of Project Akshar by admitting them to decent private institutions and funding the rest of their studies.
This project has already benefited TWENTY FIVE brilliant slum children and is looking forward for more.

AAKAR

Project Aakar is consigned for overall development of our children. Procure them with finesse in specific interests other than from academic outlook. Art, dance, music, sports, speech and more will be developed. Various activities and workshops are conducted on weekly basis for the same

ANAND

Under Aanand, educational and fun trips are organized occasionally to keep them captivated throughout the learning process. There are events, coordinated to provide them with a platform to enhance and showcase their flair in outside activities as well.

Pradaan

Under project PRADAAN we provide stationary items including books, notebooks, pens etc. Also we provide school uniforms to the students in private schools through the project Paathshala . We look for the necessities that are required for the students and provide them asap.
